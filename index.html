Biography

GUAPO is a Montreal based Producer, Composer, DJ & Guitarist. He has performed on three continents at festivals like Glastonbury (UK) and the Telerama Dub Festival (Paris). His DJ mixes regularly reach the Top 5 in the world on the Mixcloud charts.

As Music Coordinator & DJ for fashion icon Jean Paul Gaultier's Pinkarnaval & Cirque du Soleil founder Guy Laliberté's TerraKarnaval, he played to crowds of 15,000 spectators in Place Des Festivals during the Just For Laughs festival.

With Quicksound he worked with Grammy Award winner Luisito Quintero and Juno Award winner Alpha Yaya Diallo. With UK world beat pioneers Suns of Arqa he produced songs on five of their albums, working with dub legends like Youth (The Orb) & Adrian Sherwood (On-U Sound) and Indian Raga virtuosos like Ragunath Seth & Johar Ali Khan.

In his hometown he played at festivals like the Montreal Jazz Fest, Bal En Blanc, Eclipse, Timeless, Groove & Bass, Good Vibes, Era, Pop Montreal and at venues like the Metropolis, S.A.T., the Olympic Stadium, Aria, Rialto Theater etc...

As the co-founder of Flamingo Funk he booked international headliners like Krafty Kuts & Mista Trick to tour in Canada, performing closing sets after them. Combined with his epic festival closing sets at Tribunion Boreal and Khumeia, Guapo built a solid reputation for delivering unforgettable moments of joyful mayhem!

He collaborated with cutting-edge video artists and their immersive video projections in the Satosphere Dome, Lumifest, La Place Des Festival, Festival Nouveau Cinéma. Working with world renowned pioneers like Moment Factory, Gridspace, Graphics Emotion. He performed with immersive post-rock band Antennae at Nomad Nation.

Guapo also had the pleasure to work with world class circus artists like Cirque Eloize, Cirque Intime, Circo de Bakuza, Yammel Caramel, Black Mohawk and Guy Laliberté.

As a film composer, he was nominated for the Jutra Award for Best Original Score. He was the Music Supervisor on TV shows for Showcase, Universal, Netflix, IFC, YTV, CBC, Nat Geo, Discovery, Radio Canada, TQS...

Major Festivals & Closing Sets

Major Festivals

In 1995 I began performing live. In 1997 I started DJ'ing. I've played countless concerts, festivals and raves in Europe, North America and Latin America. Including trips to the U.K. to perform at one of the world's biggest festivals: Glastonbury!

Located on a 1000 acre farm in Avalon where the King Arthur & Holy Grail legends originated, Glastonbury boasts 175,000 spectators and over 100 stages with the biggest artists, bands & DJ's in the world. It is truly mind blowing!

Festivals & Closing Sets

Canada has an amazing scene for underground electronic music. It keeps you on your toes to perform for a knowledgeable crowd. No crowd will dance harder for you! It's so rewarding to be dancing together to our music outside in beautiful natural settings.

My festival closing sets at Tribunion & Khumeia and my performances after U.K. headliners like Krafty Kuts and Mista Trick have given me a reputation as a highly entertaining closer. So that the people leave on a high note with a big smile on their faces.

International Festivals

Suns of Arqa at Glastonbury & Telerama Dub Festival

Founded in Manchester in 1979, Suns of Arqa are world music pioneers, the first to combine Classical Indian Raga with Dub Reggae. Peter Gabriel discovered them in 1982, booking them at the 1st WOMAD Festival.

In 2005, I remixed one of their songs for a film and hired Moment Factory to make the music video. The success of the remix led to me joining the band. So far I collaborated on five albums and one DVD. I was the Guitarist and Tour Manager for our live performances at the Glastonbury Festival (U.K.), Telerama Dub Festival (Paris) and Musique Multi Montreal.

Avicii, Skrillex, DJ Sneak in Miami

Montreal-based Music Loves Fashion was looking for a Miami based partner to help produce a music festival there. I was working on projects with the perfect partner for them so I facilitated a relationship with Awesome Global Events. Soon after Pacha (Ibiza) and Ministry of Sound (London) joined the team.

The result was a 4 day festival featuring Grammy Award winning artists like Skrillex and Avicii. The location was at the pool of the glamorous Raleigh Hotel. Sponsors joined such as Belvédère and Moët and celebrities such as Paris Hilton and Paula Abdul attended.

Société des Arts Technologiques

India Mon Amour

As a resident performer in the S.A.T. Mix Sessions, I organized several events at this cutting edge digital art venue. My crowning achievement was India Mon Amour, which combined classical Indian music and dance with electronic musicians and video artists.

I curated the event, booking artists like Catherine Potter and tabla player Subir Dev. I performed with dancers and classical Indian musicians. I enlisted singers from l'Opéra de Montréal to perform an aria from Dido and Aeneas that I had transposed into the rhythmic structure of classical Indian Raga.

Satosphere Dome & Arcadia Festival

For Nuit Blanche 2018 I performed in the Satosphere Dome, the world's first immersive modular theatre dedicated to artistic creation. The audience are surrounded by the video projections, fully immersed in a cutting edge sensory experience. With a state of the art sound system that features 157 speakers.

The Arcadia Festival is for video game designers. For the GAMMA event, we explored the relationship between audio and the artificial intelligence of games. Eight companies created games where the action was dictated by the dynamic changes in the music. The output of each musical performance was plugged into the gaming consoles. The audience were in effect playing versus the DJ's.

Lumifest

Lumifest 2023

For the 2023 edition of Lumifest the downtown center of Repentigny was transformed with cutting edge video and light installations. The buildings were mapped with environmental video projections.

I performed a four hour DJ set in a high tech stage setup using stealth screen technology. I was completely immersed inside the video projections that were created by the talented VJ Data Dada.

Lumifest 2018

The 2018 edition of Lumifest was created with la Société Des Arts Technologiques. The downtown center of Longueil was transformed with video and light installations and the buildings were mapped with environmental video projections.

The theme was BOLLYWOOD so I performed a set of the music of India combined with electronic music. Accompanied once again by VJ Data Dada and Sinha Danse: a local dance troupe that did a Bollywood choreography.

Just For Laughs

Jean Paul Gaultier - Pinkarnaval

Quicksound was the Musical Director of this elaborate parade honouring Jean Paul Gaultier. Over 2000 costumed performers danced through the streets of Montreal. My role was to coordinate the composing & recording of the musical score, to hire the session musicians and to DJ at the event.

We hired Gridspace to create 8 videos with Jean Paul Gaultier where the music and sound fx were created by Quicksound. The videos were projected on a giant sphere during the event.

I performed as DJ Guapo in a stage shaped like a giant stiletto shoe.

Guy Laliberté - TerraKarnaval

The Cirque Du Soleil founder asked children to imagine a new world and draw what it would look like. The costumes, concepts and stage design were created based on the children's vision. My role was once again that of Musical Coordinator.

For the climax of the show, we worked with Graphics Emotion on a 15 minute sequence of environmental video projections. I created a dubstep music montage for the Apocalypse sequence and Quicksound created the music for the rebirth sequence.

As Artistic Director of the DJ's & VJ's my role was to curate the two main outdoor stages of the Festival. I selected the performers, negotiated their contracts, managed the budget, technical and hospitality riders, planned the pacing and the soundchecks. I programmed 27 VJ performances and 42 performances by Montreal's top DJ's.

Felix Leclerc - TerraKarnaval 2013

Mentored by Robert Charlebois this event paid tribute to the iconic Félix Leclerc. Quicksound was the Musical Director. In my role as Musical Coordinator, I facilitated the composing and recording of 8 remixes of his work and the creation of a parade and elaborate show in La Place des Festivals.

We received the old master recordings of Félix Leclerc on analog two inch tape and hired a special team to restore and digitize them. Quicksound then transformed his songs into musical styles like Tango, Afro Beat, Drumline, Circus and House Music.

Quicksound feat. Luisito Quintero

As Production Director, I coordinated the creation of a 2 hour concert in La Place Des Festivals in front of 20,000 spectators. For the first hour Quicksound performed featuring special guest Luisito Quintero from Venezuela. Luisito has won over 15 Grammy Awards with Tito Puente, Celia Cruz, Little Louie Vega, Herbie Hancock etc...

For the 2nd half of the show I performed as DJ Guapo accompanied by the sexy robot dancers of Black Mohawk and renowned aerial artist & Guinness World Record holder Yammel Caramel.

Flamingo Funk

Krafty Kuts

In 2023 I co-founded the Flamingo Funk crew with DJ Melodrastik to create events for the latest underground sounds in Bass Music. Our first event was to bring UK Breakbeat pioneer Krafty Kuts to Montreal to perform. Renowned for his turntablism and original productions, he is a seminal force in the UK's Breakbeat and Bass scene.

We created flamingo avatars for all the performers, postered the city and decorated the venue with flamingos. The audience came dressed in flamingo apparel. I had the honor of doing the closing set right after Krafty Kuts and it was a massive success! Bass Face in full effect!

Mista Trick

In 2024 Flamingo Funk teamed up with Tesla Nights and with bass music crews in multiple cities. We booked Mista Trick from Bristol (UK) for a mini tour of Quebec and Ontario. He is in a league of his own as a DJ & Producer, mixing vintage, vaudeville vibes with down and dirty drum n bass.

He revolutionized the sound of electro swing music and founded the Swing & Bass label that has taken the world by storm. A prolific producer & remixer he also performs live with a full band the Mista Trick Collective.

Once again I had the honor of performing the closing set and it was pandemonium!

Antennae

On Halloween 2023, I joined experimental post rock band Antennae to create immersive concerts for Nomad Slow TV, broadcast on ROKU. We created improvised soundscapes surrounded by video projections with mirrors reflecting it all back.

"Piki Chappell caressed the cello while Guapo's atmospheric guitar painted the night with shadow and light. Karina Marquez Caballero's vocals soared with spectral wings. Dan Handelman's guitar strummed beauty. Quentin Noël Bourbeau's fingers danced on the piano keys. John Juster's saxophone sang with the voice of an autumn wind. Together, they crafted an immersive sonic journey of rhythm and vibration."

The project and all the video projections were created by Jason Rodi, the co-founder of Moment Factory and the founder of Nomad.

Radio Shows

Since 2022 I host shows for Radio La Face B. I began by creating a world music show called Global Grooves. Each episode I explored the musical traditions of a culture and combine it with electronic music. So far I explored the music of India, Brazil, Latin America, West Africa, North Africa, the Middle East and Asia Minor. I currently host the show Pépites the third Thursday of every month at 9 pm.

Since 2023 I have been a guest host on CKUT 90.3 FM for shows hosted by the highly respected DJ Andy Williams. I create a show the first Sunday of every month from 2pm-4pm for The Magic Roundabout. I create DJ mixes exploring underground bass music.

Record Label

Alpha Yaya Diallo

In 2013, I was the production director for the creation of an album & music video by Montreal based producer Quicksound with the famous African musician Alpha Yaya Diallo.

Discovered by Peter Gabriel, Alpha's music is a blend of the Malenke, Sousou and Foulani cultures. 20 years ago he moved to Canada and he has since won four Juno Awards. He sang on the score to the hit film "District 9".

Ubisoft

While working for Quicksound, we were hired to conduct the A & R talent search to find the singer who will launch the record label of video game company Ubisoft.

We proposed 5 candidates including the winner, a Norwegian artist named Anja. She recorded several singles for the best selling Just Dance franchise.

All the candidates I proposed went on to have amazing careers. They included Sheenah Ko, Annie Sama, Belle Scar and Grimes, who six months later became a huge international star signed to the British label 4AD.

Finite Records

In 2001 I founded Finite Records. We were an eclectic label that had artists in genres like Jungle, Breakbeat, House, Techno, Ambient, Drum n Bass, Latin Lounge, Alt Rock, Alt Country, and Doom Metal.

One of the songs from our ambient artist Peabody was featured on the Montreal Independent Music Awards compilation album. Songs from our artists were licensed to many feature films, documentaries and TV shows.

I created a jungle breaks solo album as Lowkey and a post rock ambient EP under the name Cephalopod. With Peter X we created an EP of Breakbeat house as Saul Paul. As Guapo I created an album of Latin & Brazilian House, Drum n Bass & World Beat.

Pure Soundtrack CD

As the Composer and Music Supervisor of the film "Pure", I was the Production Director and Artistic Director of the soundtrack CD. I placed emerging electronic artists from Montreal alongside popular artists from Europe.

I launched the album on my label Finite Records and negotiated international distribution. I organized the launch at le Rendez Vous Du Cinéma Québécois. DJ Maus, Jan Pienkowski and I performed a set with VJ's from Moment Factory.

Feature Film & Television

Feature Film

In 2001 I founded Finite Music, a studio and record label that specialized in music for films & TV. We were a team of composers, music supervisors and music editors. For my first feature film I received a nomination for the 2002 Jutra Award for Best Original Score.

For "3 Saisons", I worked with popular Québécois actors like Caroline Néron and Romano Orzari. The film won a multitude of awards internationally.

For "The Eye in the Son" by Moment Factory, I used the sound of footsteps on snow, wind and heavy breathing to re-create the environment of Mont Everest. For the film Pure I had to re-create the environment of afterhours electronic music clubs, the trippy musical journey the DJ takes you on.

Television

I also work as a Music Supervisor. My role is to define the musical direction, hire the score musicians, oversee the score creation, handle the music licensing, create and manage the soundtrack CD, book bands and DJ's to perform on camera etc... I have negotiated over 500 music licensing contracts.

My biggest project was Family Biz, a 26 episode series for YTV. For Urban Myth Chillers and The Business I had the good fortune of working with Hollywood actors like Omar Sharif and Kathleen Robertson.

I worked on documentaries for National Geographic and Discovery Channel as well as fictional shows like Jack Of Diamonds for Showtime and 21 Thunder for CBC. For the Radio Canada series Le Clan, I developed a musical style we named Shediac Gothic: a combo of Americana, Trip Hop and Industrial Music.
